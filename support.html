FAQ

  • What is the difference between A1-PRO and A1-ULTRA?

  • PRO Specifications: Speed: 17mph / 28km/h Range: 10 miles / 16km Charging: 1.5A charger, approximately 2 hours charging time

  • ULTRA Specifications: Speed: 20mph / 32km/h Range: 15 miles / 24km Charging: 3A supercharger, approximately 1 hour charging time

  • Difference: ULTRA includes a 3A supercharger, 2 ankle supports, 8 straps, and LED wheels.

  • Can I bring AIRTRICK on an airplane?

  • Yes, all models can be taken on board as carry-on items. They should be placed in your backpack or small carry-on luggage. You can also bring them on the subway or trains.

  • What is the waterproof rating? Can I ride in rainy conditions?

  • We do not recommend riding in rainy conditions as the wet surface may cause slippage. The product has an IPX-3 waterproof rating, which means it is not designed to be submerged in water. Water damage may lead to short circuits and product damage.

  • Can the battery be easily removed?

  • No, the battery is built-in and cannot be quickly detached. It requires unscrewing the front wheel and battery box to disassemble the battery, which is not designed for rapid removal.

  • What shoe sizes are available? Are they one size fits all?

  • The shoes are one size fits all, suitable for US sizes 5-14 (male) and 6-14 (female).

  • What is the maximum weight capacity?

  • The maximum weight capacity is 240lbs / 110kg.

  • Can I purchase spare parts after the warranty period?

  • Yes, spare parts are available for purchase in our accessory store page. If you cannot find the specific parts you need, please contact our online customer service or email airtrick@airtrick.top.

  • Can I glide without electric power?

  • Yes, you can manually glide like regular rollerblades or roller skates even when the power is depleted.

  • Is it difficult to learn how to use these e-skates?

  • If you have previous rollerblading experience, you can generally learn within 15 minutes. However, if you are a beginner with limited balance, we recommend patience and practice after making a purchase. For more guide videos please check the AIRTRICK YouTube channel https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCOPhrz2P0iewMxRphttp4uQ

  • Does it have a suspension system?

  • The product primarily relies on the elasticity of the front and rear wheels for shock absorption. Both wheels are made of elastic PU material, with strong shock absorption.

  • Is it shipped by sea or air?

  • During the checkout process, you can choose between sea and air shipping based on your preferences and requirements.

  • How long does it take to ship after purchase?

  • Generally, we ship within 1-2 business day after the purchase is made.

  • How do I control the speed of the e-skates? What is the slowest speed?

  • The speed is controlled by the handheld remote controller. The remote has a throttle, pushing it forward accelerates, and pulling it backward applies brakes. The remote offers four speed modes: LO (up to 3mph / 5km/h), MI (up to 6.2mph / 10km/h), HI (up to 12mph / 20km/h), and PRO (up to 17mph/28km/h). The throttle is gradual, allowing for smooth acceleration from a standstill.

  • How do I brake?

  • AIRTRICK e-skates are equipped with dual braking systems: electronic brakes on the remote controller and rubber stoppers at the heel part.

  • How do I power on and off the AIRTRICK e-skates?

  • To power on, simply slide the rear wheel to activate with two audible beeps. The e-skates will automatically power off after 3 minutes of inactivity. There is a small switch button located near the charging port, which requires a small tool to press. A single click powers on the e-skates, and another click powers them off. Generally, this button is not frequently used in regular operation.
